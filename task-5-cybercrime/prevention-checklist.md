## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never scan QR codes to receive money  
- Do not share UPI PIN or OTP with anyone  
- Verify payment requests before accepting  
- Avoid clicking unknown links  
- Use strong passwords and secure apps  
- Enable two-factor authentication  
- Regularly check bank statements  
- Report fraud immediately on cybercrime.gov.in  
- Call helpline number 1930 for assistance
